Splender

Web & Mobile

Goal

Improve the online shopping experience and encourage people to begin their shopping journey at Splender.

About

Customers earn cash back when they shop at 800+ stores (Nordstrom, Gap, etc.) through Splender.

User Research

My first task with Splender was to work with the product manager to identify areas to improve the shopping experience of Splender. Weekly, we consulted with beta testers from our target audience and learned that people wanted to search for products on Splender, not just stores.

UX Design

To create a product search feature, I began by reviewing recent e-commerce usability studies conducted by Baymard. These studies tested and analyzed everything from sorting, filtering, searching, and displaying products on e-commerce websites. Using this knowledge, I found the best examples of each of these features and designed Splender product search.

Final Prototypes

After showing sketches to the beta testers, I created a high-fidelity clickable prototype. I also created and began testing responsive, mobile, versions of the Splender webpage. After numerous iterations, I presented the final prototypes to the engineering, legal, and marketing teams.

Mobile App

During the process of improving the Splender website, I also began designs for a Splender mobile app. Unlike the website, the app focuses more on favoriting and creating a more personalized experience for the Splender user.
