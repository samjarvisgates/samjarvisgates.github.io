Trend

Mobile App

Goal

Make it easier to discover cool new fashion trends and help small fashion designers gain recognition.

About

A combination of Instagram and Pinterest, Trend lets users post and view new clothing and accessories.

User Research

Trend targets consumers who are in their twenties. These consumers tend to be on Instagram and often use Pinterest to find cool clothing as well as Wanelo, a popular social shopping site. After talking to some Wanelo shoppers, I decided to enhance the social networking aspects of Trend to foster a stronger community and keep users coming back.

UX Design

After creating some initial sketches, I developed the wireframes for Trend in Adobe Illustrator. The wireframes featured a tabbed navigation bar rather than a hidden "hamburger-style" menu to encourage users to interact with all the application features. I also designed Trend to mimic the look and feel of Instagram, making it familiar and easy to use for anyone who has used Instagram.

Initial Prototype

From feedback I recieved on the wireframes, I learned that users wanted the app to be less text-heavy and that they wanted more emphasis on the products. Incorporating this information, I developed an inital high-fidelity prototype that hides the comments and likes and highlights the name of the product.

Final Prototype

I continued to iterate on the initial prototype, sending it to friends and compiling more feedback. I am now currently developing an initial IOS version of Trend. Before the final development, I hope to test it with a larger audience and understand more precisely how people will use it.
